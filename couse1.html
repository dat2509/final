Places I Want to Visit Once in My Life

1. Ha Long Bay - Vietnam

Ha Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for thousands of islands rising dramatically from the emerald water.

2. Lavender Field - Provence, France

In summer, Provence is filled with purple colors and the sweet fragrance of lavender.

3. Yosemite Falls - California, USA

One of the tallest waterfalls in North America, located in Yosemite National Park.

Travel Preparation Checklist

  • Passport and visa (if traveling abroad)
  • Camera or smartphone with good camera
  • Light jacket and sunscreen
